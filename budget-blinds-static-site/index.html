Motorized
Window Shades

With Budget Blinds motorized shades make light control effortless. Our shades bring style, convenience, and smart technology to your home, even on hard-to-reach windows.

Request a Free Consultation

Motorization allows you to easily adjust shades on hard-to-reach windows or in rooms with lots of natural light, all with the touch of a button.

Program your shades to open and close at specific times of day, creating the perfect ambiance for every moment while maximizing energy efficiency.

Cordless motorized shades eliminate dangling cords, creating a safer environment for children and pets while maintaining a clean, streamlined appearance.

Create the appearance of an occupied home by scheduling your shades to open and close while you're away, enhancing security and peace of mind.

Automated shades help regulate indoor temperature by blocking heat in summer and retaining warmth in winter, reducing energy costs year-round.

Features Include

  • Sync your shades with your smart home system and control them by voice. Compatible with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, Apple HomeKit, and more.

  • Control your motorized shades effortlessly with convenient wall-mounted or handheld remotes. Perfect for any room in your home.

  • Manage your shades from anywhere using our intuitive mobile app. Set schedules, create scenes, and control individual or grouped shades with ease.

  • Enjoy manual operation with elegant wand controls that complement your motorized system. The perfect backup and aesthetic touch.
